Off the Court

Aside from my educational accolades referenced above, I pride myself on my interpersonal skills and ability to work well with others. I've always believed that grades will get you a job, but your likeability and work ethic will help you keep your job. If I had to choose one quality about myself that I believe sets me apart from the next person, it would be my emotional intelligence. Being able to speak with people of all different ethnicities and intelligence levels, and then relate to and work in lockstep with such people is something I've always been able to do without much friction or resistance.
